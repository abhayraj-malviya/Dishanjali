Dishanjali is a Non-Governmental organisation dedicated to the cause of helping underprivileged children educate themselves and promote the significance of education among the poor.

WHAT WE DO?

Dishanjali was started in the year 2013 by a group of social workers, including Er. Rajeev Baghel and Er. Vijay Kumar Patidar who realised that the purpose of knowledge is not to store it within oneself but to share it, especially with those who are willing to gain knowledge but do not have suitable means to acquire it. Today Dishanjali has many education centres in the cities of Indore, Bhopal and Vidisha, which run on a weekly basis and involve participation from various university students across the cities.

We at Dishanjali aim to do the following through the means of education:
  • To return back smiles on these heavenly adorable faces,
  • To return them the with days filled with learning and enjoying,
  • To hand them the sword of education by which they can fight against the competitive world,
  • To givethem a better future.

With a team that is a blend of both enthusiastic,freshers and experienced veterans, this dream does not seem too far. The core values of the organisation further provide direction to these individuals so that they can turn this dream into reality. Dishanjali endeavours to provide an ambience to its members where they have the freedom to share their opinions and ideas. For this purpose,events arer egularly organized to allow team members to connect with each other and together comeup with more creative ideas to make the lives of these children better.
