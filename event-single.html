About Event

What is Manthan Talks?

Respected Dr. Anoop Swaroop Sir (Vice Chancellor Jagran Lakecity University Bhopal) patron of Dishanjali and Mr.Vikas Saxena(Mentor Dishanjali) has taken an initiative of making a think tank and the name of think tank is #manthan, which is shaping up as Manthan Talks.

Manthan talk is a platform to talk and nurture the solutions of the problems. Since we are a part of the society and if we are not solving the problem, then perhaps constructive discussion can bring us to a solution. Manthan is started keeping in mind how important discussion and discourse is to have conversations and try and try until we solve a problem.

We're building an engaging platform powered by people and thought leaders, offering a full spectrum of problem-led solutions that allows us to relate with our audience/experts & our community. At Manthan, we plan for a self-sustainable system so that every expert will support the other, & bring a positive change in society.

At Manthan, we plan on churning ideas in a free, frank & constructive way. Which means deep contemplation, discussions of facts and numbers,ideas and thoughts across diverse issues and nations and analysis aimed at a :

  • 1. Solution
  • 2. Projection (an estimate of a future situation based on a study of present).

We are Going to have Manthan Talks On Regular Intervels like Ted Talks Or Josh Talks.
